Daily Self-Care

  • Start each day with gratitude
  • Maintain consistent sleep schedule
  • Move your body for 30 minutes
  • Connect with someone you care about
  • Take breaks from digital devices
  • Practice saying "no" to protect your energy
🧘

Mindfulness Techniques

  • 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding: Name 5 things you see, 4 you hear, 3 you touch, 2 you smell, 1 you taste
  • Body Scan: Notice sensations from head to toe
  • Mindful Walking: Focus on each step
  • Loving-Kindness: Send good wishes to yourself and others
